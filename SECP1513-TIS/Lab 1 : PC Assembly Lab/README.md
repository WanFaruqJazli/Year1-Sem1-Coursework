Our first lab focused on the practical side of computer hardware. Following a detailed demonstration by the lecturer on safe handling techniques, we worked in groups to disassemble and reassemble a Personal Computer. ## ðŸ”§ Suggestions for Improvement
* **Increase Practical Time:** Allocate more duration for the hands-on portion so every student has the chance to handle more components individually.
* **Additional Toolsets:** Provide extra sets of tools to minimize waiting time during collaborative group work.
* **Troubleshooting Exercise:** Introduce a brief segment focused on solving common issues (e.g., identifying why a PC won't boot) to build real-world problem-solving skills.
* **Procedural Checklist:** Supply a quick guide or checklist for students to review before starting and upon completion of the lab.
